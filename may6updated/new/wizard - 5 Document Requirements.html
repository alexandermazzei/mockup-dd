Define Document Requirements

Customize the documents you'll need for this due diligence project. Required documents are pre-selected based on your project scope, but you can add or remove items as needed.

Financial Documents

Documents related to financial statements, projections, and tax records.

Annual Financial Statements (3 years) Required
Audited income statements, balance sheets, and cash flow statements.
Quarterly Financial Statements (Current Year) Required
Unaudited quarterly financial reports for the current fiscal year.
Financial Projections Required
Forward-looking financial forecasts and budget plans.
Tax Returns (3 years) Required
Federal, state, and local tax filings for the past three fiscal years.
Debt Schedule Optional
Detailed breakdown of all outstanding debt obligations and payment terms.
Capital Expenditure Schedule Optional
Historical capex spending and planned future capital investments.
Accounts Receivable Aging Report Optional
Current A/R aging with details on outstanding receivables.

Legal Documents

Corporate formation, contracts, intellectual property, and compliance documentation.

Articles of Incorporation Required
Official company formation documents filed with the state.
Bylaws Required
Internal rules governing the company's operations and management structure.
Material Contracts Required
All significant contracts with customers, vendors, and partners.
Intellectual Property Documentation Required
Patents, trademarks, copyrights, and trade secrets documentation.
Litigation Documentation Optional
Records of ongoing or threatened legal proceedings and settlements.
Regulatory Filings Optional
Industry-specific regulatory submissions and compliance documentation.

Operational Documents

Information about company operations, infrastructure, and business processes.

Organization Chart Required
Current organizational structure showing departmental relationships.
Employee Handbook Optional
Company policies, procedures, and employee guides.
Key Customer Agreements Required
Contracts with top customers, including terms and conditions.
Vendor Agreements Optional
Contracts with key suppliers and service providers.
Property Leases Optional
Real estate leases and property documentation.
Insurance Policies Optional
Business insurance coverage details and policy documentation.

Technology Documents

Documentation related to IT systems, software, and digital infrastructure.

Technology Stack Overview Required
Complete list of technologies, frameworks, and platforms used.
Software Licenses Required
Inventory of all licensed software and associated agreements.
System Architecture Diagrams Optional
Technical diagrams showing the infrastructure architecture.
IT Security Policies Optional
Security protocols, policies, and compliance documentation.
Technology Roadmap Optional
Future technology plans and development initiatives.
Data Center Information Optional
Details on data center facilities and infrastructure.

Market & Industry Documents

Information about market positioning, competition, and industry trends.

Market Analysis Reports Optional
Reports analyzing the relevant market size, trends, and growth potential.
Competitor Analysis Optional
Detailed analysis of key competitors and competitive positioning.
Industry Reports Optional
Third-party research and industry analysis.
