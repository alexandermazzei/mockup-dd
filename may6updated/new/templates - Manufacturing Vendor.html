About This Template

The Manufacturing Vendor template is designed to streamline the due diligence process for evaluating and onboarding manufacturing suppliers, focusing on key considerations related to production capabilities, quality management, regulatory compliance, and supply chain resilience. This template provides a structured framework for assessing potential manufacturing partners, identifying operational risks, and ensuring alignment with your organization's manufacturing requirements.


Use this template when your organization is considering new manufacturing vendors, re-evaluating existing suppliers, or conducting periodic audits of manufacturing partners. The template includes specialized assessment modules to evaluate production capabilities, quality control processes, compliance documentation, and overall manufacturing fitness.
