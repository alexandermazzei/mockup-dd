High Risk

Debt-to-EBITDA ratio has increased to 2.8x, up from 2.4x last year, approaching the covenant threshold of 3.0x. The company's current trend line indicates potential covenant violation within 6-9 months without corrective action.

  • Limited access to additional capital if covenants are breached

Risk Mitigation Recommendations
Prioritized Action Plan
High Priority Actions (Next 90 Days)
  1. Customer Diversification Strategy: Develop and implement a structured program to reduce top customer dependency from 45% to below 30% of revenue within 18 months. Begin with targeted sales efforts in adjacent markets.
  2. Succession Planning: Formalize succession plan for CEO/Founder including knowledge transfer protocols and relationship transition plans for key clients.
  3. Debt Covenant Management: Proactively engage lenders to renegotiate covenant thresholds or implement cash conservation measures to avoid potential breach.
  4. FX Hedging Program: Implement formal currency hedging program to reduce EUR exposure using forward contracts with 6-12 month horizons.
Medium Priority Actions (Next 180 Days)
